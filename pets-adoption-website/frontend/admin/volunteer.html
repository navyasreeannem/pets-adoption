How Our Volunteering Program Works

Our pet volunteering program connects pet owners who need temporary care for their pets with loving volunteers who can provide a safe home environment.

1. Submit Your Request

Fill out our volunteer request form with details about your pet, the dates you need care, and any special instructions.

2. Get Matched with a Volunteer

Our team reviews your request and matches your pet with a suitable volunteer based on experience, location, and availability.

3. Meet and Greet

Arrange a meeting with the volunteer to ensure they're a good fit for your pet and discuss care details.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pet volunteering program.

Is there a fee for using the volunteer service?

Our volunteer service is completely free. We connect pet owners with volunteers who are happy to help care for pets out of their love for animals. However, pet owners are responsible for providing food, medication, and any other supplies needed for their pet's care.

How are volunteers screened?

All volunteers go through a thorough screening process that includes background checks, home visits, and reference checks. We also require volunteers to have prior experience with pets and provide training on pet care best practices.

How far in advance should I request a volunteer?

We recommend submitting your request at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure we have enough time to find a suitable match for your pet. For last-minute requests, we'll do our best to accommodate but cannot guarantee a match.

What happens in case of a pet emergency?

All volunteers are trained to handle basic pet emergencies and have access to your emergency contact information. We also recommend providing your vet's contact information and any specific instructions for emergency situations.

Can I request a specific volunteer?

Yes, if you've worked with a volunteer before and would like to request them again, you can mention this in your request form. We'll check their availability and try to accommodate your preference.
